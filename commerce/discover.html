Downtown

The downtown area is home to dozens of small businesses. You'll find everything from food to crafts here in this homey area.

Parks

Bring the little ones to the numerous fun parks scattered throughout the boundaries. No two parks are the same, so make sure to hit them all for endless fun.

Rec Center

Play basketball, tennis, volleyball or swim, run and climb at Nampa's Rec Center. With a wide variety of classes, there is something for everyone!

Lake Lowell

Splash into fun at Nampa's favorite summer spot! With 14.5 square miles of lake to explore, you can take your pick of fishing, swimming, or boating.

Deer Flat National Wildlife Refuge

Come take a look at the protected land and animals in the wildlife refuge. There's even a visiter center with activities to help you and the young ones learn more about what goes on in the refuge.
